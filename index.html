Our Story · আমাদের গল্প

A Name Trusted
by Krishnanagar

Started in 1990 at Patra Bazar, Bichitra Biswas Vegetable Shop has grown from a single stall to one of the most trusted vegetable suppliers in Krishnanagar. We serve homes, hotels, restaurants, dhabas, and schools — all with the same commitment to quality and freshness.



Located at M.M. Ghosh Street, Patra Bazar — we are your neighbourhood's most reliable source for farm-fresh vegetables, every single day.
