Our Heritage · আমাদের ঐতিহ্য

A Name Trusted
by Krishnanagar

Started in 1990 at Patra Bazar, Bichitra Biswas Vegetable Shop has grown from a single stall to one of the premier vegetable suppliers in the region. We serve homes, fine-dining restaurants, local dhabas, and institutions — all with the same commitment to uncompromising quality and daily freshness.


Located at M.M. Ghosh Street, Patra Bazar, we are your reliable partner for farm-fresh produce, every single day.
